The Engine Mechanics

The core typing engine utilizes a custom predictive layer optimized for the Devanagari script.

Haptic Feedback Emulation

Every keystroke triggers a subtle visual and auditory response modeled after the physical resistance of a 1960s mechanical typewriter. This "digital weight" creates a rhythm that encourages consistent speed.

Dynamic Ligature Mapping

Complex Nepali conjuncts are rendered in real-time. Our engine intelligently predicts trailing matras and halants, ensuring that the visual representation is as fluid as the typing intent.

Tactile Soundscapes

Choose between 'The Obsidian' (deep, muted thuds) or 'The Silver' (crisp, high-frequency clicks). Each sound profile is binaurally recorded for maximum immersion.
