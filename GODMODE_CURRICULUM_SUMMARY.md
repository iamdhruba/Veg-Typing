# 🎯 GODMODE CURRICULUM - Complete Professional Typing Mastery

## Overview

A comprehensive, scientifically-structured curriculum for the Nepali Preeti Keyboard that takes learners from absolute beginner to professional expert typist (50+ WPM with 99% accuracy).

## Curriculum Structure

### **PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Levels 1-10)**

**Goal**: Build muscle memory and keyboard familiarity

#### Level 1-3: Home Row Mastery

- **p1**: Left hand home row (a s d f)
- **p2**: Right hand home row (j k l ;)
- **p3**: Complete home row alternation

#### Level 4-7: Reach Key Expansion

- **p4**: Top row left (q w e r)
- **p5**: Top row right (y u i o)
- **p6**: Bottom row left (z x c v)
- **p7**: Bottom row right (b n m , .)

---

### **PHASE 2: CONSONANT MASTERY (Levels 11-40)**

**Goal**: Learn all 33 Nepali consonants organized by linguistic groups

#### Velar Group (11-12)

- **p8**: क ख (k Shift+K)
- **p9**: ग घ (g Shift+G)

#### Palatal Group (13-14)

- **p10**: च छ (c Shift+C)
- **p11**: ज झ (j Shift+J)

#### Retroflex Group (15-16)

- **p12**: ट ठ (q Shift+Q)
- **p13**: ड ढ (x Shift+X)

#### Dental Group (17-18)

- **p14**: त थ (t Shift+T)
- **p15**: द ध (d Shift+D)

#### Labial Group (19-20)

- **p16**: प फ (p Shift+P)
- **p17**: ब भ (b Shift+B)

#### Semi-Vowels & Sibilants (21-22)

- **p18**: य र ल व (y r l v)
- **p19**: श ष स ह (Shift+S z s h)

#### Nasals (23)

- **p20**: न म (n m)

---

### **PHASE 3: MATRA MASTERY (Levels 41-55)**

**Goal**: Master vowel modification signs

#### Basic Matras (21-25)

- **p21**: आ matra (f key)
- **p22**: इ ई matras (i Shift+I)
- **p23**: उ ऊ matras (u Shift+U)
- **p24**: ए ऐ matras (e Shift+E)
- **p25**: ओ औ matras (o Shift+O)

#### Diacritical Marks (26-27)

- **p26**: Anusvara & Visarga (+ Shift+M)
- **p27**: Halant/Virama (/)

---

### **PHASE 4: CONJUNCT MASTERY (Levels 56-75)**

**Goal**: Master complex consonant combinations

#### Simple 2-Consonant Conjuncts (28-31)

- **p28**: गुत्तुरल conjuncts (k/k k/y k/r)
- **p29**: Dental conjuncts (t/y t/r t/t)
- **p30**: Dental nasal conjuncts (n/t n/y n/d)
- **p31**: Double consonants (d/D d/y p/r)

#### Complex 3+ Consonant Conjuncts (32-34)

- **p32**: क्ष त्र ज्ञ (k/z t/r j/y) - Most important
- **p33**: श्र (Shift+S/r) - Sibilant + liquid
- **p34**: Double retroflexes (x/x q/q)

---

### **PHASE 5: VOWELS & SYMBOLS (Levels 76-95)**

**Goal**: Master standalone vowels, numbers, and punctuation

#### Standalone Vowels (35-37)

- **p35**: अ आ (c cf)
- **p36**: इ ई (b[ b[[)
- **p37**: उ ऊ (b' b\")

#### Numbers (38-39)

- **p38**: ० १ २ ३ ४ (0-4)
- **p39**: ५ ६ ७ ८ ९ (5-9)

#### Punctuation (40)

- **p40**: । , . ? !

---

### **PHASE 6: FLUENCY & SPEED (Levels 96-115)**

**Goal**: Build natural typing flow with real content

#### Basic Vocabulary (41-42)

- **p41**: Common words (high-frequency)
- **p42**: Simple phrases

#### Intermediate Vocabulary (43-44)

- **p43**: Education domain
- **p44**: Society & nature

#### Advanced Vocabulary (45)

- **p45**: Professional terminology

#### Sentence Drills (46-48)

- **p46**: Easy sentences
- **p47**: Medium complexity
- **p48**: Complex literature/news

---

### **PHASE 7: GODMODE - PROFESSIONAL MASTERY (Levels 116-125)**

**Goal**: Achieve professional typing speeds with perfect accuracy

#### Constitutional Language (49)

- **p49**: Official government documentation

#### Complex Conjunct Challenge (50)

- **p50**: Text packed with difficult combinations

#### Speed Trial (51)

- **p51**: Target 50+ WPM with 99% accuracy
- Mixed authentic Nepali text
- Real-world typing scenarios
